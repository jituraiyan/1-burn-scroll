How fast can your body burn calories? How fast does your body burn fat?

Your metabolic rate determines how quickly your body can turn the food you eat, and your stored fat, into energy. If you have a slow metabolic rate, losing fat will be damn-near impossible.

Green tea leaf extract has been found to support metabolic rate which helps burn fat. It has also been found to boost antioxidant levels

Green tea has compounds called epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG). EGCG helps an enzyme that normally breaks down norepinephrine. Norepinephrine is a neurotransmitter that plays a key role in regulating metabolic rate and as a result, fat burning.
